Top Destinations

Simlipal National Park

Home to tigers, elephants, and lush forests.

Satkosia Gorge

A stunning river gorge with crocodile habitats.

Debrigarh Sanctuary

Rich biodiversity by the Hirakud Reservoir.

Jagannath Temple

The famous temple in Puri with grand Rath Yatra festival.

Konark Sun Temple

13th-century architectural marvel built as a colossal chariot for the Sun God, Surya.

Chilika Lake

A large, pear-shaped, brackish water lagoon on India's eastern coast in Odisha.

Nandankanan

A unique combination zoological park and botanical garden.

Hirakud Dam

A multipurpose river project in Odisha, India, built on the Mahanadi River and inaugurated in 1957..

Lingaraj Temple

A historic and architecturally significant Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.
